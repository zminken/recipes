Pancake Recipe (Breakfast for 2)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Ingredients:
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
3/4 cup milk
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 egg
2 tablespoons melted butter
2 tablespoons sugar
1 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp vanilla
1/8 tsp cinammon
Honey (to taste)


Directions:
~~~~~~~~~~~~
1. Add vinegar to milk, and let sit for 5 min.
2. Combine dry ingredients in mixing bowl.
3. Mix remaining wet ingredients into milk/vinegar.
4. Combine wet ingredients and mix with for or hand mixer until no lumps.
5. Cook in skillet on medium heat.
